MakeYour Mark InA ReimaginedLandmark

Discover prewar apartments transformed by award-winning CetraRuddy Architecture at the corner of 79th Street and West End Avenue.

Built in 1927, 393 West End Avenue embodies the legacy of the Upper West Side. Like the vibrant neighborhood it inhabits, 393 is a remarkable mosaic of past and present. A storied building that preserves prewar style while offering contemporary comfort. Introducing the future of 393 — a new heritage.
